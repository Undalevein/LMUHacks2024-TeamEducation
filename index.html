⚠️ Housing Application Closes Thursday!
Reminder, the 2024-2025 Student Housing application and room selection process is underway. Any currently enrolled spring 2024 undergraduate student is eligible to apply. Applications will close tomorrow, Feb 22nd at 5:00 p.m. PST.

To access, please log on to MyLMU and select Campus Life > Student Housing Portal > On-Campus Housing Application. To complete the housing application and participate in the next phase of the process, Room Selection in late March, you must complete your housing application no later than 5 p.m., Thursday, Feb. 22.

Once you have submitted a complete application, you will receive additional information regarding the next steps of the housing selection process. Please be sure to review the Student Housing website to become familiar with the process.

If you have any questions, our office is available at housing@lmu.edu or 310-338-2963.
